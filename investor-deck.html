Slide 1 | Vision

Affordable personal VTOL with mission-ready flexibility.

FLYABOV is building a practical electric manned drone platform powered by COREfx dual-mode propulsion for utility, rescue, and recreational deployment.

Market position: today we execute in Rural Air Mobility (RAM) for off-road and rural operations. We plan to enter Urban Air Mobility (UAM) air taxi markets as regulation becomes clearer.

20 min Target Flight Time

Slide 2 | Problem

Current options force a tradeoff between accessibility and capability.

High Cost Barrier

Most manned aerial platforms are priced beyond personal and small-team adoption.

Mission Rigidity

Single-mode systems are less adaptable across open-air and close-quarters tasks.

Limited Field Utility

Many platforms are optimized for demos, not real utility and rescue workflows.

Complex Ownership

Operators need compact, configurable aircraft with clearer operating boundaries.

Slide 3 | Product

COREfx dual-mode propulsion defines the platform.

Exposed Propeller Mode

Best for: open-air operation and responsive handling in non-confined environments.

Constraint: not intended for close-quarters operation.

Electric Ducted Fan (EDF) Mode

Best for: close-quarters maneuvering and controlled enclosed-thrust flight.

Value: mission adaptability across utility, rescue, and recreation.

Market Path
